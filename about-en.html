About DOEL S.Q

Commitment to Quality, Confidence at Every Step.

DOEL S.Q is a reference point in the meat storage and distribution industry, serving as a reliable bridge between quality producers and businesses. With experience focused on the highest food standards, we ensure that the products we handle arrive at the customer with uncompromised freshness.

More Than Just Logistics

At DOEL S.Q, we don't just manage goods — we build sustainable partnerships. By investing in modern refrigeration technology and a fast distribution fleet, we give businesses the confidence they need to succeed.

  • HACCP Standards: Storage and monitoring according to international norms.
  • Efficient Logistics: Fast delivery to your points of sale.
  • Integrity: Full transparency at every stage of cooperation.

Production

State-of-the-Art Technology

We use the most modern certified technology on the market. Food safety and cleanliness are our non-negotiable standard.

Cold Storage Rooms

Our meat is stored in refrigeration rooms with automatically controlled temperature and humidity, preserving maximum nutritional and hygienic values.

Hygienic Processing

Our processing system operates with minimal excess contact and fully sterilized cutters that eliminate any risk of contamination. Ingredients that never disappoint.

European Certifications

Every process within our factory is audited and certified in accordance with the latest ISO and HACCP standards, exporting uncompromised quality.
