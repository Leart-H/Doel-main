FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you do wholesale supply?

Yes, we are primarily focused on wholesale sales for restaurants, supermarkets, and other business entities. We make flexible supply contracts according to the specific requirements of traders.

Where are your retail points of sale located?

Our stores are located in main centers and various local strategic points. Retail fresh product sales are available from morning until late evening.

Do you have vegetarian/plant-based products?

Our current catalog is strictly specialized toward essential meat categories, with an emphasis on beef, chicken, and pork, along with specialized sausage and salami blends.

How long do wholesale deliveries take?

After signing a contract with a minimum volume, deliveries will operate 2 to 3 times per week, or daily depending on your district or branch. All movement is done with properly maintained refrigerated transport.
